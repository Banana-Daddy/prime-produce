The Desk

Two principals.
One standard.

Prime Produce was founded in 1999 by Rick Harkinson and Marc Nelson, and has run on the same handshake ever since: call the grade you see, ship the load you called, settle on the terms you signed.

The business is quiet by design. No storefront, no retail tail, no middle‑layer of brokers routing to brokers. When a buyer calls, one of the two principals picks up. When a grower ships, the paper follows the truck. When a carrier pulls, the settlement is on the agreed schedule. Twenty‑six years of that adds up to a record that speaks for itself.

“Supply from Mexico, the East Coast, and Chile continues to move — weather patterns shift, but the obligation to deliver on spec does not.”

Marc Nelson, Partner · Blue Book Services, 2016
